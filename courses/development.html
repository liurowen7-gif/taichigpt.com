Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to complete the Foundation course first?
Recommended but not required. If you have prior Tai Chi or martial arts experience, you may start here.
What is the 24-form sequence?
The 24-form is the core sequence of the Xin Yi Hun Yuan system, created by Grandmaster Feng Zhiqiang. It encodes the complete principles of Chen-style Tai Chi in a flowing practice.
Will I learn push hands?
Yes. This course introduces single-hand push hands fundamentals — the beginning of partner practice.
Can I practice alone?
Absolutely. The forms and exercises are designed for solo practice. Push hands concepts are taught with visualization techniques for solo training.
